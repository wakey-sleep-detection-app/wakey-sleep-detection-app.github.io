About the app

Wakey is an Android app that prevents you from falling asleep while driving. It tracks your eyes and makes an alarm once it detects fatigue symptoms.

FAQ

Do I need the internet in order to use Wakey?

  • No, there’s no need tp be connected to the internet.

What else do I need, besides my smartphone?

  • Besides a smartphone, you just need a regular smartphone handle.

Does Wakey! collects personal data?

  • No, since the application functions offline Wakey! doesn’t collect any data.

Can I stay listening to music via another app while using Wakey!?

  • You definitely can, Wakey! works in the background
